If you dread shopping for weekly groceries because you simply don’t have the time or patience,
we can take care of it all. We take orders on 24/7 and our delivery team will also provide 24/7 services to you.

The Process

Once you place your order, our shoppers team starts collecting your items. We will note any
items you order from our perishable departments, and send those orders to our different department heads.
A produce manager will select your produce, your meat will be cut fresh by the butcher that morning,
and your deli order will be filled right before delivery. We pride ourselves on the freshest products at competitive prices.
No boxes, paper or plastic bags to deal with We bring your order in special packs that keep items safe and cool,
without excess packaging. You get exactly what you ordered, but nothing to clean up. You pay the same,
low prices you enjoy at your local Walmart grocery, every day of the week.
